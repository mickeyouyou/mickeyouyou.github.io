◆ REGISTER_ADAPTER

#define REGISTER_ADAPTER (   name)

Macro to prepare all the necessary adapter functions when adding a new input/output. For example when you want to listen to car_status message for your module, you can do REGISTER_ADAPTER(CarStatus) write an adapter class called CarStatusAdapter, and call EnableCarStatus(car_status_topic, true, callback(if there's one)) in AdapterManager.
